Where to get help

Police

Call 112 in case of an emergency - for example, if you are in immediate danger to your life or safety.

For less urgent situations, where the perpetrator is no longer present, call 114 to report violence or threats.

You can also go in person to a police station to report the crime.

Source: politi.dk

Free lawyer service

If you need free legal advice, the Duty Lawyer Service offers short consultations with a lawyer.

You can ask questions about any type of legal issue.

You may choose to remain anonymous.

When: Tuesdays from 16:00–18:00 (closed during school holidays and between Christmas and New Year).

Where: Aalborg Main Library, Rendsburggade 2 9000 Aalborg - you can draw a number for the queue between 15:30–17:30.

How: The service is first come, first served and cannot be booked or contacted by phone.

What: You will receive oral advice only - the lawyer does not prepare documents or handle cases.

Source: aalborgbibliotekerne.dk
